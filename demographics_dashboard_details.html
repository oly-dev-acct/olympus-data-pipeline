3. KPI Scorecards & Formulas

#ScorecardFormula / QueryNotes
1 Total Residents Demographics.objects.count() Filtered by RLS + user-selected filters
2 Average Age Avg('age') Also shows Min and Max age range; nulls excluded
3 Avg Annual Income Avg('current_employment_estimated_annual_income') Nulls excluded; shown in gold color
4 Employment Rate employed_count / total_residents × 100 Employed = records where current_employment_name is not null/empty
5 Lease Signers filter(lease_signer='Yes').count() Sub-label shows % of total residents
6 Avg Ledger Balance Avg('ledger_balance') Per resident account; nulls excluded; shown in blue
